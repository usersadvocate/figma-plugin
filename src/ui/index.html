🎯 What does this linter do?

❌ The Problem

Your design files often mix design tokens (consistent values) with arbitrary values (hardcoded numbers), making your design system inconsistent and hard to maintain.

❌ Inconsistent
Spacing: 16px (token) + Padding: 12px (arbitrary)

✅ The Solution

This linter scans your selected frames and shows you exactly where you're using design tokens vs. arbitrary values, helping you maintain consistency.

✅ Consistent
Spacing: 16px (token) + Padding: 16px (token)

🚀 Benefits

  • 🔍 Find inconsistencies in your design system
  • 📊 See coverage of design token usage
  • 🎯 Identify areas that need tokenization
  • ⚡ Maintain consistency across your designs
